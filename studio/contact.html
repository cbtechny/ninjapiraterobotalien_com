Frequently Asked Questions

When will Sackjack Island be released?

We're currently in active development and don't have a release date yet. Follow our devlogs for regular updates on progress and milestones.

We're developing with PC as our primary target, with web demos available for browser play. Additional platforms will be announced as development progresses.

Absolutely! We'll be sharing playable demos and prototypes through our website. We value community feedback and incorporate it into development.

We're always interested in connecting with talented artists, developers, and creators. Reach out via email with your portfolio and what you're interested in.
